# IR265 — Depreciation Rates

## Overview

The IRD publishes approved depreciation rates for different asset types. Businesses must use these rates (or apply for a special rate) when calculating tax depreciation.

## Methods

### Diminishing Value (DV)
- Rate applied to the opening book value each year
- Higher deduction in early years, decreasing over time
- Most commonly used method

### Straight Line (SL)
- Rate applied to the original cost each year
- Equal deduction each year over the asset's life
- Lower rate than DV for the same asset

## Low-value assets

Assets costing less than $1,000 (GST-exclusive) can be fully expensed in the year of purchase rather than depreciated. This threshold was increased from $500 to $1,000.

## Common rates

### Computers & electronics
| Asset | DV Rate | SL Rate | Life |
|---|---|---|---|
| Desktop/laptop/tablet | 50% | 25% | 4 years |
| Computer monitor | 50% | 25% | 4 years |
| Printer | 40% | 20% | 5 years |
| Server | 50% | 25% | 4 years |
| Mobile phone | 67% | 33.5% | 3 years |

### Office equipment
| Asset | DV Rate | SL Rate | Life |
|---|---|---|---|
| Desk | 20% | 13% | 10 years |
| Office chair | 20% | 13% | 10 years |
| Filing cabinet | 13% | 8.5% | 15 years |

### Motor vehicles
| Asset | DV Rate | SL Rate | Life |
|---|---|---|---|
| Car/van/ute | 30% | 21% | 5 years |
| Motorcycle | 30% | 21% | 5 years |
| Trailer | 20% | 13% | 10 years |

### Building fit-out
| Asset | DV Rate | SL Rate | Life |
|---|---|---|---|
| Carpet | 25% | 16% | 8 years |
| Blinds/curtains | 40% | 20% | 5 years |
| Signage | 40% | 20% | 5 years |

## Disposal

When an asset is sold or scrapped:
- **Sale price > book value**: Depreciation recovered (taxable income), capped at original cost
- **Sale price < book value**: Loss on sale (deductible expense)
- **Scrapped ($0)**: Full remaining book value is a loss on sale

## Key rules
- An asset can never be depreciated below zero
- Depreciation is pro-rated for the number of months owned in the tax year
- Buildings with an estimated useful life of 50+ years cannot be depreciated (since 2012)
- The IRD Depreciation Rate Finder can be used to look up rates for specific assets
