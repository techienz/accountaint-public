# GST Overview

Goods and services tax (GST) is a 15% tax on most goods and services sold or consumed in New Zealand. If you carry on a taxable activity and your turnover is or is expected to be more than $60,000 in a 12-month period, you must register for GST.

You can voluntarily register if your turnover is below $60,000. Once registered, you must charge GST on your taxable supplies and can claim GST on your business expenses.

## Filing frequencies
- **Monthly** — turnover over $24 million, or by choice
- **Two-monthly** — default for most businesses
- **Six-monthly** — turnover under $500,000 and approved by IRD

## Accounting basis
- **Invoice basis** — account for GST when you issue or receive an invoice
- **Payments basis** — account for GST when you pay or receive payment (available if turnover under $2 million)
- **Hybrid basis** — invoice basis for sales, payments basis for purchases
