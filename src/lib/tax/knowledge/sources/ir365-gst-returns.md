# Filing GST Returns

GST returns are due on the 28th of the month following the end of the taxable period, with two exceptions:
- **Period ending 31 March** → return due **7 May**
- **Period ending 30 November** → return due **15 January**

If the due date falls on a weekend or public holiday, the return is due the next working day.

You must file a return even if you have no GST to pay or claim (a "nil return").

## Late filing
A late filing penalty of $50 applies if the return is filed late (first offence). Repeated late filing can result in higher penalties. Interest is charged on any unpaid GST from the due date.

## Key boxes on the GST return
- **Box 5** — Total sales and income (GST exclusive)
- **Box 6** — Zero-rated supplies
- **Box 9** — Total purchases and expenses (GST exclusive)
- **Box 13** — GST to pay or refund due
