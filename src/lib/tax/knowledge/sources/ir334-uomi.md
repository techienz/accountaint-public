# Use of Money Interest (UOMI)

IRD charges use-of-money interest on underpaid tax and pays interest on overpaid tax. The rates are different:
- **Underpayment rate**: currently around 10.91% (check IRD for current rate)
- **Overpayment rate**: currently around 3.33% (check IRD for current rate)

## When UOMI applies
- On income tax underpayments from the due date of each provisional tax instalment
- On GST underpayments from the return due date
- On PAYE underpayments from the payment due date

## Avoiding UOMI
- Use the **AIM method** for provisional tax — no UOMI if you follow AIM correctly
- Pay provisional tax on time and estimate accurately
- **Safe harbour** — no UOMI on third instalment if RIT is under $60,000 and first two instalments were paid on time using the standard method
- **Tax pooling** — buy tax from a pooling intermediary to cover shortfalls (effective date can be backdated)

## Voluntary disclosures
If you discover an error, making a voluntary disclosure before IRD contacts you may reduce penalties.
