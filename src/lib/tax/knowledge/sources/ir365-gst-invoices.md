# Tax Invoices

To claim GST on purchases, you must hold a valid tax invoice from the supplier.

## Full tax invoice (purchases over $1,000)
Must include:
- The words "tax invoice"
- Supplier's name, address, and IRD number
- Date of issue
- Description of goods or services
- Quantity or volume
- Total amount charged (including GST)
- GST amount charged

## Simplified tax invoice ($50 – $1,000)
Must include:
- Supplier's name and IRD number
- Date
- Description of goods or services
- Total amount including GST

## Buyer-created tax invoices
In some cases, the buyer can create the tax invoice (e.g., in auction situations). Both parties must agree to this arrangement.

## No tax invoice needed
- Purchases of **$50 or less** — no tax invoice required
- Certain supplies like coin-operated machines, public transport
